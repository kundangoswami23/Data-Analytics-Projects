# 📊 Business Insights

## 📌 Overview

This report summarizes the key findings from the Blinkit Sales Analysis project.

---

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Sales
- Average Rating
- Top Selling Category
- Top Performing City

---

## 📈 Business Insights

### 🛒 Sales Performance
The sales data helps identify high-performing product categories and outlets.

### 🏙 City Performance
Comparing sales across cities helps understand regional demand.

### 📦 Product Categories
High-selling categories contribute the most to overall revenue.

### ⭐ Customer Rating
Customer ratings indicate product quality and customer satisfaction.

### 📅 Monthly Trend
Monthly analysis helps identify seasonal sales patterns.

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Increase stock for high-selling products.
- Improve marketing in low-performing cities.
- Focus on improving customer ratings.
- Monitor monthly sales trends for better planning.
